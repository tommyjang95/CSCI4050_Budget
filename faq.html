BudgetUp

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is BudgetUp?
A: BudgetUp is a web application made for, but not restricted to college students.

Q: Why should I use BudgetUp?
A: BudgetUp provides an easy-to-use interface with a modern design, while giving an accurate representation of the user's spending money.

Q: What kind of budget plans does BudgetUp provide?
A: BudgetUp features an rollover budgeting plan that works similarly to cellphone plans. BudgetUp will also give an option for the user to create their own budgeting plan.

Q: Is it free?
A: Yes!
